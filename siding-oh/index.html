Fiber cement siding is becoming an increasingly common option for homeowners looking for both strength and visual appeal. At Perfect-Fit Siding Installation Services in Ohio, OH, we specialize in installation of this innovative material, which combines the sturdiness of cement with the flexibility of fiber reinforcement.

A major benefit of fiber cement siding is its long-term longevity. Engineered to handle even the most harsh weather conditions, it resists UV rays, moisture, and temperature shifts. With proper care, fiber cement siding can last up to 50 years, making it a wise investment over time.

Insulated siding combines the aesthetic appeal of regular siding with an added layer of rigid foam insulation, providing exceptional energy efficiency and durability.

One of the primary advantages of insulated siding is its impressive energy-saving potential. Insulated siding can lead to considerable heating energy savings, with studies showing up to approximately 8% reduction in energy consumption when normalized for outdoor temperature variations. This means lower utility bills and a more comfortable living environment year-round.

But the benefits go beyond energy savings. Insulated siding also offers excellent noise reduction properties, creating a serene home environment. The additional insulation layer acts as a sound barrier, reducing external noise from traffic, neighbors, or other sources.

Furthermore, insulated siding boasts increased impact resistance compared to traditional siding. The rigid foam backing provides an extra layer of protection against hail, debris, and other potential damage, ensuring your home's exterior looks great for years to come.
